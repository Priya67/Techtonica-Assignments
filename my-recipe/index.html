Tahchin-Saffron Rice


Traditional persian dish Tahchin with Saffron and Barberries is on of the oldest and finest persian dish or just a persian classic delicacies! It is a rice dish, mostly prepared with chicken, however some may prefer meat, veggies or plain as a side dish.

* Photos credit: I got it from my Maman

👩🏽‍🍳Ingredients (3 servings)

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup barberries
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground safron

⏰Prep Time: 30 mins

⏰ Cook Time: 2 hrs

🥗Prepration

  1. Bring a med-pot of water to boil, once boiled add rice with 2 large palmfuls of salt (about ½ cup).
  2. Let rice cook in boiling water for 6–8 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  3. Melt butter in skillet, add barberries, saute berries then add rose water and suger to the pot.
